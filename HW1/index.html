A Game Memory

One of my strongest gaming memories comes from playing Roblox- specifically, Dandy's World, with a couple of friends of mine. We had been fiending to reach Floor 20, which is rare for most players of the game to accomplish. And not only did we do that, we reached Floor 25.

We achieved it through teamwork and connection. I remember how excited we were when we finally reached our goal like it had happened yesterday.
